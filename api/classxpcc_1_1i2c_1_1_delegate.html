Detailed Description

For true asynchronous operation, the communication driver should inherit from this class, allowing multistage driver design and performance gain by premature data evaluation. Be aware the methods may or may not be called during the I2C interrupt even before returning from initiating function. This Delegate will stay attached to I2C during whole operation.
